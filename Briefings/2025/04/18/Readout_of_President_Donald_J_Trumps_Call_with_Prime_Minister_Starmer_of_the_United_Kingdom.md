# Readout of President Donald J. Trump’s Call with Prime Minister Starmer of the United Kingdom

Today, President Donald J. Trump held a call with Prime Minister Keir Starmer of the United Kingdom. The two leaders discussed bilateral trade, ongoing talks to bring the conflict in Ukraine to a peaceful resolution, and regional security in the Middle East. President Trump shared he looks forward to his upcoming State Visit with His Majesty King Charles III in the United Kingdom later this year.
