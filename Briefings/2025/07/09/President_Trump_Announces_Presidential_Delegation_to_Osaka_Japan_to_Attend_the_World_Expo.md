# President Trump Announces Presidential Delegation to Osaka, Japan, to Attend the World Expo

President Donald J. Trump today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Osaka, Japan, to attend the World Expo on July 19, 2025. The Honorable Secretary Scott Bessent of the United States Department of the Treasury will lead the delegation. **Members of the Presidential Delegation:** The Honorable George Glass, Ambassador of the United States to Japan The Honorable Lori Chavez-DeRemer, Secretary of the United States Department of Labor The Honorable Christopher Landau, Deputy Secretary of the United States Department of State The Honorable William E. Grayson, Ambassador, USA Pavilion Commissioner General, Expo 2025
