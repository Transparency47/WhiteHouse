# Congressional Bills S.J.Res. 80 Signed into Law

On Friday, December 5, 2025, the President signed into law:

S.J.Res. 80, which nullifies a Bureau of Land Management rule relating to “National Petroleum Reserve in Alaska Integrated Activity Plan Record of Decision.”
