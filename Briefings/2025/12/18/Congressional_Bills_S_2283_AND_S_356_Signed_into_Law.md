# Congressional Bills S. 2283 AND S. 356 Signed into Law

On Thursday, December 18, 2025, the President signed into law:

S. 2283, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 201 West Oklahoma Avenue in Guthrie, Oklahoma, as the Oscar J. Upham Post Office; and

S. 356, the “Secure Rural Schools Reauthorization Act of 2025,” which reauthorizes the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self-Determination Act of 2000 through fiscal year 2026.
