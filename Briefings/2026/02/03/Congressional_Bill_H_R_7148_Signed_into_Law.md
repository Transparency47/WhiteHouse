# Congressional Bill H.R. 7148 Signed into Law

On Tuesday, February 3, 2026, the President signed into law:

H.R. 7148, the “Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2026,” which further makes consolidated appropriations for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2026, and for other purposes.
