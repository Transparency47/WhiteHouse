# Congressional Bill H.R. 7147 Signed into Law

On Thursday, April 30, 2026, the President signed into law:

H.R. 7147, the “Homeland Security and Further Additional Continuing Appropriations Act, 2026,” which makes further consolidated appropriations for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2026, and for other purposes.
