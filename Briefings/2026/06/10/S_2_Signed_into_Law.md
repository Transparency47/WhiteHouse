# S. 2 Signed into Law

On Wednesday, June 10, 2026, the President signed into law:

S. 2, the “Secure America Act,” which provides funding to the Department of Homeland security, U.S. Customs and Border Protection, and U.S. Customs Enforcement through Fiscal Year 2029 for immigration enforcement and related activities.
