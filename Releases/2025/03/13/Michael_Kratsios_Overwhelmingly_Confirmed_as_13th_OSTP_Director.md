# Michael Kratsios Overwhelmingly Confirmed as 13th OSTP Director

**WASHINGTON, D.C. –** On Tuesday, March 25 th, Michael Kratsios was overwhelmingly confirmed as the 13 th Director for the White House Office of Science and Technology Policy in a 74-25 vote.

Here’s what industry leaders and key administration officials are saying:

*“Congrats to [@MKratsios47](https://x.com/MKratsios47) on his Senate confirmation as Director of the White House Office of Science & Technology Policy. With [@POTUS](https://x.com/POTUS) and the NEDC we will drive innovation, fuel the AI revolution, and empower America’s next Golden Age!”* [**Secretary of the Interior Doug Burgum**](https://x.com/SecretaryBurgum/status/1904602515330466003)
