**The criminal illegal immigrant is *already* home — he’s a Salvadoran citizen.**

Where was the Maryland senator’s concern when one of his own constituents, Rachel Morin — an innocent mother of five — was brutally murdered by an illegal immigrant? Van Hollen [didn’t even bother](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/1912470477081018717) to contact her family.

Van Hollen didn’t say a word about the radical policies that put his constituents at risk.
