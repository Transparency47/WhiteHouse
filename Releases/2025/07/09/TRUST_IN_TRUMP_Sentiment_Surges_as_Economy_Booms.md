# TRUST IN TRUMP: Sentiment Surges as Economy Booms

When President Donald J. Trump unveiled his historic America First [trade agenda](https://www.whitehouse.gov/fact-sheets/2025/04/fact-sheet-president-donald-j-trump-declares-national-emergency-to-increase-our-competitive-edge-protect-our-sovereignty-and-strengthen-our-national-and-economic-security/) in April, he [offered some free advice](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/114296801411259400): “Don’t be Weak! Don’t be Stupid! Don’t be a PANICAN … Be Strong, Courageous, and Patient, and GREATNESS will be the result!”

**President Trump was right (again).**

- **Wall Street sentiment is surging as the stock market reaches new** [**record**](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/1938702910986317825) [**highs**](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/1939781868200595882) **.** [JPMorgan Chase & Co.](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-06-06/jpmorgan-u-turns-on-stock-market-now-sees-slight-gain-for-2025), [Goldman Sachs](https://archive.ph/rEX6F), [Citigroup](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/06/09/citigroup-joins-chorus-of-wall-street-banks-and-hikes-sp-500-target.html), [Deutsche Bank](https://www.reuters.com/business/finance/deutsche-bank-lifts-sp-500-year-end-target-amid-wall-street-upgrade-wave-2025-06-03/), [UBS Global Wealth Management](https://www.reuters.com/business/finance/ubs-lifts-sp-500-indexs-annual-target-fading-trade-tensions-2025-06-27/), and [Barclays](https://www.reuters.com/business/barclays-lifts-sp-500-year-end-target-6050-2025-06-04/) have all elevated their S&P 500 Index year-end targets — now predicting record gains to continue.

- **Consumer outlook is on the rise.** With core inflation at its [lowest level](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2025/06/under-president-trump-america-is-defeating-inflation/) since March 2021, expectations for an inflation spike have “ [completely receded](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/07/08/inflation-expectations-drift-back-down-to-pre-tariff-levels-new-york-fed-survey-shows.html) ” as Americans [grow](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2025/05/consumer-confidence-surges-majority-says-were-on-right-track/) [increasingly](https://www.cnn.com/2025/06/13/economy/consumer-sentiment-may) [confident](https://www.reuters.com/business/us-consumers-happier-about-finances-expect-stable-inflation-new-york-fed-says-2025-07-08/) about the economic outlook.

- **Price hikes never materialized.** A [new study](https://www.whitehouse.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/Imported-Goods-Have-Been-Getting-Cheaper-Relative-to-Domestically-Produced-Goods.pdf) found that prices for imported goods have actually declined *faster* than prices for overall goods — confirming that tariffs have had little effect on prices.

- **By all metrics, President Trump is overseeing another economic boom.** Jobs numbers have trounced expectations for [four straight months](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2025/07/june-boom-jobs-report-shows-the-economy-continues-to-soar-under-president-trump/), gas prices remain at their [lowest level](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/06/28/gas-prices-will-be-the-cheapest-theyve-been-in-years-expert-says.html) in four years, and blue-collar wage growth has seen the [largest increase](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2025/06/icymi-blue-collar-wage-growth-sees-largest-increase-in-nearly-60-years-under-trump/) in nearly 60 years.
