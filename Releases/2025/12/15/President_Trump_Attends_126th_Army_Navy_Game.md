# President Trump Attends 126th Army-Navy Game

**On Saturday, President Donald J. Trump attended the historic 126th Army-Navy football game at M&T Bank Stadium in Baltimore, Maryland, marking his sixth appearance at this cherished American tradition.** As Commander-in-Chief, President Trump joined thousands of Cadets, Midshipmen, and other Patriotic fans to celebrate the unparalleled dedication, discipline, and valor of the United States Military Academy and Naval Academy teams.

President Trump participated in the ceremonial coin toss on the field, engaged with the enthusiastic crowd, and stood proudly with our great service members during the national anthem and military flyover — a display of the deep respect and unwavering support this Administration has for our nation’s armed forces.
