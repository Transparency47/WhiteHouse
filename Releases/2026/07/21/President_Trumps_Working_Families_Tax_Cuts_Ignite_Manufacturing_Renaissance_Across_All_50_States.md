# President Trump’s Working Families Tax Cuts Ignite Manufacturing Renaissance Across All 50 States

**One year after President Donald J. Trump signed the Working Families Tax Cuts into law, a** [**new report**](https://nam.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/NAM_Tax_Wins_Collection_Final.pdf) **confirms the legislation has delivered a powerful** [**manufacturing resurgence**](https://www.foxbusiness.com/economy/manufacturers-say-gop-tax-law-protected-jobs-preserved-wages-economic-growth-across-every-state) **in every state.**

The landmark law has [sustained](https://nam.org/issues/tax/) nearly six million American jobs, preserved more than $1 trillion in economic output, and safeguarded $540 billion in wages — among the strongest one-year impacts on U.S. manufacturing in modern history.

Through bold, pro-growth policies — including full expensing for equipment and machinery, immediate R&D expensing, full deductions for new and expanded factories, and strong incentives to build in America — the Working Families Tax Cuts are fueling investment, innovation, and growth across the entire manufacturing sector.
